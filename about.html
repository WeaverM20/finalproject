There are many interesting facts about Mount Washington itself that make it a unique adventure, and helps set it apart from the other mountains in the area.

About

  1. The peak of the mountain is 6,288.2ft
  2. The location is Sargent's Purchase, Coos County, New Hampshire
  3. It is known as the highest peak in the Northeastern United States
  4. The Appalachian Trail crosses the summit of the mountain
  5. It is also located within the Presidential Range of the White Mountains
  6. The mountain is also known for its weather due to the unpredictable and wild weather

Activities

  1. Hiking
  2. snowboarding
  3. skiing
  4. snowboarding
  5. biking
  6. sledding/tubing
  7. racing
  8. guided tours
  9. camping

There are multiple trails that you can use to go up the mountain that vary based on hiking experience, which also means they may take longer. In the winter season there are paths where people can go snowboarding, skiing, sledding, tubing, and snowshoeing. Camping at the top of Mount Washington is not available due to the high winds and wild weather, but yoyu are able to camp on the way up the mountain. There is a museum at the top of the mountain which is provided by the Mount Washington Observatory that tells you about their work, and the history of the mountain. hiking and climbing are an activities that you can engage in that are available through the seasons, but are exceptionally harder in the winter. There are also some popular events that happen that are road races where people climb up the mountain and race to the top. There is another popular racing event that happens where cars race from the base of the mountain up to the summit.

Ways to travel up the mountain

  1. Car
  2. train
  3. hiking
  4. climbing

When going up the mountain there are multiple options for you to pick from on how to get up to the top. The quicker options are by car or train since you it can take you up the mountain within less than an hour. If you do not feel comfortable driving up the mountain the auto road service provides a van service that brings you up, and in the winter they also offer snowcoach tours that takes 1.5 hours. If you do not want to drive up the mountain or take a car there is also the train optiont that is offered from Mount Washington COG Railways in 45 minutes, which is also available in the winter.
